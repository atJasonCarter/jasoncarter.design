Analytics / Data Visualization

User Experience/ UI

Simplified
Break-down

The unique Analytics TrueData has is one of its main selling points. After building an Audience, or uploading First-party data, users can turn on the ability to see the breakdown of the audience by Demographics, App ownership, Locations visited, or Hardware characteristics. I completely redesign the reporting to make charting more robust, flexible, and future-proof. I also did discovery testing to find what analytics were most useful to our customers, and find what statistics we didn't currently had that we needed to get to provide value. I also created the process to make our charts and anlytics actionable so that users could compare charts, and build Audiences directly from the Vizualizations.
