Consent Management Platform

Product Design

Users should have control over their data. Users should be able to easily say what companies have their data and what data those companies have. While at TrueData I was luckily enough to sit on a team of people that helped influence data regulations in the United States towards user protections. As data regulations come into play, apps that collect data either incidentally or out of necessity need to comply with these regulations to protect end consumers. At TrueData we wanted to help apps be compliant without necessarily needing to hire developers and legal teams to build out expensive Consent Management. Additionally the users of these apps need to be aware of their choice and be able to easily choose if they wish to give consent to use their data. The two main regulations that were designed for are GDPR in Europe and CCPA in California.

Enabling A/B Testing

Our product was designed to solve the European regulation, GDPR. To allow all end-users to give informed consent, we translated a basic consent message in the 23 most common languages found in Europe. We set the default language to English, but enabled users to change what the default language would be (e.g. a German app would most likely use Deutsch). We also enabled companies to make a short, meaningful plea to their end-users asking for consent.

Configuring Data Sharing Partners

A Data Partner is any company or entity that has access to your end-user's data. This could be for advertising purposes, a sister company, analytics, data trading/selling, etc. Our product allows end-users to opt-in and give affirmative consent to data partners.

1/3
1

Type to search. Company names were pulled from two APIs, A public company tracker, and the IAB compliancy list. For companies that appear on neither, users can continue to type to add a custom partner.

2

98.27% of all data partners are 8 advertising partners. We made sure they could be added with a click.

3

In order for TrueData to show companies analytics on how their CMP is performing, TrueData needs to be added as a data partner. Hovering over 'Free Analytics' gave customers a visual preview of analytics they would receive.
